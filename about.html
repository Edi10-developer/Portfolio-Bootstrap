Bio

I have been working as a barman for several years during which I developed my creative and communicative skills. During these years in the hospitality industry, also I had the chance to lead different teams. This definitely helped me develop strong organizational as well as time-management skills. In addition, thanks to the nature of my job, I had the chance to travel and meet (as well as work) people from all sorts of background. These experiences definitely thaught me how to quickly adapt to new social environment and allowed me to develop further my outgoing personality. In 2018 I discovered the world of programming and how creative one can be while trying to develop a website.. Since then, I am now devoting all my energy to learning and improving as much as I can.

where I was born

I was born in 1991 in the city of Durres, a coastal town in Albania. There’s where I first fell in love with the sea. At the age of 6 my family moved to Italy. I speak Albanian at native level.

where I grew up

At the age of 6 I arrived in Italy, in the city of Sacile, located about one hour away from Venice. I grew up and lived through my teenage years there. Then I found out that I loved to travel and after few short-term work experiences, in 2017 I moved to Milan for 1 year to gain some professional experience. I speak Italian at native level.

where I live

In 2018, at the age of 26, I moved to Barcelona, ​​Spain, finally rediscovering my love for the sea. I have lived here for almost 3 years now and this is where I discovered my passion for Front-End programming. I speak Spanish at advanced level.
