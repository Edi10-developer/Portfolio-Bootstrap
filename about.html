About me

Bio

I worked as a barman for several years where I developed creative and communicative skills that represents me. I had the opportunity to manage various work teams and situations of high stress. My previous job (the barman) allowed me to travel, meet and work with people of different cultures and I learned the meaning of the word adapt. I've always been an outgoing type. In 2018 I discovered the world of programming and how creative it can be. Since then I have devoted all my energy to learning as much as I can.

where I was born

I was born in Albania in 1991 in the city of Durres. Being a coastal city I fell in love with the sea. At the age of 6 my family moved to Italy. I speak Albanian at native level

where I grew up

At the age of 6 I arrived in Italy, in the city of Sacile, located about one hour from Venice. I grew up there and lived through the teenage years. Discover that I liked to travel and after various short experiences in 2017 I moved to Milan for 1 year to grow professionally.

where I live

At the age of 26, in 2018 I moved to Barcelona, ​​Spain, finally rediscovering my love for the sea. I have lived here for almost 3 years and this is where I discovered the world of Front-End programming
